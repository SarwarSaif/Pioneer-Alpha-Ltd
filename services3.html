About

Amar iSchool is a live classroom platform. Here students can communicate with industrial expert mentors directly in an online classroom platform if they enrolled into the mentor’s courses. Academic courses are not always enough for the students. Even if they taught skill development courses themselves by watching online videos, they can’t be evaluated properly.

Vision

Our Vision is provide skill development courses online according to Bangladeshi job market . Here mentors will follow adaptive curriculum based methods to deliver courses . Students can only pay for the part of the courses they’ve watched. They can avoid the redundant parts they have already known. We’ll provide certifications for the students who completed their tasks properly instructed by mentors. Also other online course platforms are too expensive and not in our mother tongue. Amar iSchool provides skill development courses at low cost and in Bengali. A graduate student will have much better portfolio than before. She /He would be confident and skilled enough to get Job opportunities.
